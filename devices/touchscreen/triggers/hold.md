# TouchscreenHoldTrigger
:::{list-table}
* - **Inherits**
  - [](/devices/touchscreen/triggers/index)

* - **Action events**
  - ``begin``, ``cancel``, ``end``, ``tick``, ``update``

* - **Finger count range**
  - 1-5

* - **Type**
  - Time-based trigger
:::

Performed by placing fingers on the touchscreen and not moving them for 200 ms.
