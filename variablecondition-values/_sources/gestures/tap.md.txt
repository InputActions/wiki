## Description
Performed by quickly putting down fingers and lifting all of them. 

Supports up to 5 fingers. Tap-to-click must be enabled in order for 1-3 finger tap gestures to work.

Requires [libevdev backend](<project:/devices/touchpad.md#libevdev-backend>).
